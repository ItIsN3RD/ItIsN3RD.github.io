The Beginning

Mojos started as a small computer repair shop in Shawano WI. In 2003 we expanded to a much larger building in Shawano to sell all of the things that brought people so much joy. Shortly after we opened a store in Antigo. In 2015 we opened our Oshkosh location. Our newest stores are located in the Bay Park Mall in Green Bay and inside the Fox River Mall in Appleton! Never in our wildest dreams did we imagine we would get this far with such humble beginnings. None of this would have been possible without your contribution! Thank you all from the bottom of our hearts for sticking with us through this crazy thing called life.
